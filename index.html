About MonkeyKing Project

Sun Wukong, also known as the Monkey King or Great Sage Equal to Heaven, is one of the Seven Great Sages and arguably the most famous fictional character in Chinese literature. He is a mythical figure depicted as a skilled practitioner of magic, a monk, and a formidable warrior with the appearance of a monkey. The character is based on folklore dating back to the Tang Dynasty.

In the novel "Journey to the West," Sun Wukong is born from a stone and acquires supernatural powers through training under a Daoist master. After rebelling in Heaven and being imprisoned by the Buddha beneath a mountain, he is eventually freed and accompanies the Buddhist monk Tang Sanzang on a journey to retrieve sacred scriptures from the West.

Token Specifications

HOGE is an ERC20 token on the Ethereum blockchain. It’s designed to be deflationary, burning away at itself with every transaction. There were no team tokens allocated at launch, and the project today is 100% community owned and operated. There are no developer wallets or marketing wallets, meaning the community rallies together to raise funds, thus making HOGE a pure DeFi project.

As part of the deflationary aspect of the token, a 2% burn and redistribution tax applies to every transaction. That tax is split between a burn function (forever removing tokens out of circulation), and a redistribution action that gives back to the holders. That’s it. No taxes for marketing, team, or dev wallets exist.
